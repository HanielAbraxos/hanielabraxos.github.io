Speech to Sign Language Converter

Artificial Intelligence

The AI Speech to Sign Converter is an innovative assistive technology project designed to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and the Deaf or hard-of-hearing communities.

This system uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing (NLP) to translate spoken language into sign language in real-time, providing an intuitive and accessible way for Deaf individuals to understand spoken communication.

Speech Recognition

AI Model

The Speech Recognition AI is a cutting-edge technology designed to convert spoken language into written text with high accuracy and speed.

Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and deep neural networks, this AI system can recognize and transcribe speech in real-time, making it a powerful tool for a wide range of applications such as virtual assistants, transcription services, voice-controlled interfaces, and more.

The project aims to provide a versatile and scalable solution that can adapt to different languages, accents, and speech patterns, ultimately enhancing user interaction and accessibility.
